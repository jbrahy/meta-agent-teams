# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This project exists to make AI agent development more transparent, auditable, and ethical. We are committed to providing a welcoming and constructive environment for everyone who wants to contribute to that goal.

## Standards

**Constructive behavior includes:**

- Sharing what you've learned from real-world use of the framework, including failures
- Providing specific, actionable feedback on others' contributions
- Raising safety concerns about constitutions, guardrails, or agent behavior — even if it's uncomfortable
- Helping newcomers understand the architecture and contribution process
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully and offering it kindly

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory language directed at individuals
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Dismissing safety concerns without substantive engagement
- Deliberately introducing harmful patterns into agent prompts or constitutions
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces — issues, PRs, discussions, and any other communication channels associated with the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior can be reported to the project maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality regarding the reporter.
